The W6 District is comprised of Downtown Austin and Old West Austin. DOWNTOWN - Congress Avenue cuts throught the center of Downtown from Lady Bird Lake to the Capitol. Downtown is split into several entertainment districts including:  6th Street, 2nd Street, Rainey Street, Market District, Seaholm District, and Red River District. Downtown is also home to the convention center, Brackenridge Hospital, and the Frank Erwin Center. OLD WEST AUSTIN - Old West Austin is one of the City's oldest neighborhoods. It contains a number
of houses that are more than 100 years old, some of which are designated historic
landmarks. Commercial and office uses are concentrated on Lamar, 5th Street, 6th Street, and
West Lynn. Some residential structures are now used as offices, especially on 5th Street, 6th
Street, and Baylor.
