<p>District N8 is in central North Austin and composed of the Brentwood, Highland, Crestview, and Wooten neighborhoods. These neighborhoods were annexed by the city in the mid-20th century. In 1979, The Planning Commission recommended converting US 183 into a freeway as a result of increased traffic. Construction to convert US 183 into a freeway began in 1994. This district is served by CapMetro's MetroRapid, MetroRail, on top of the standard bus routes. Transit Oriented Developments are located along the MetroRail line at the Highland and Crestview stations.
<p>
The 2015 population estimate of the district is 22,519. Most of its housing units are renter-occupied. 
</p><p>
Unlike other neighborhoods in Austin, the neighborhoods in this district have not experienced the same rate of growth in recent years.
