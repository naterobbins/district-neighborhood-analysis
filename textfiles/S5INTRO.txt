William Cannon Drive is the northern border of S5. The district does not extend further east than IH 35 nor does it extend further west than the Union Pacific Railroad. S5 is home to Mary Moore Searight Metropolitan Park as well as Southpark Meadows Shopping Center. There are now over 25,000 people that call S5 home. The district's average houshold income is around $70,000. There are just over 5,000 owner occupied housing units in S5. There is roughly the same number of Hispanic residents is the district as there are White residents. Other races and ethnicities make up a small portion of the S5's population. Over half of the districts cases are Property Abatement cases and over 25% are Land Use cases. Almost 90% of the complaints in S5 are of the Neighborhood variety.
