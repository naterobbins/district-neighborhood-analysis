<p>District W1 surrounds the intersection of FM 620 and US 183 Highway. It is home to many shopping centers, including Lakeline Mall. The population of the district is roughly 44,000 people. The number of owner-occupied households in W1 is slightly higher than the city average. Average household income in this district is approximately $88,000. Over two thirds of the W1's residents are White, and nearly 20% are Hispanic. Property-abatement cases tend to be more common than land-use cases, and there is actually a significant number of structure-condition cases. Almost 80% of the cases in W1 are neighborhood cases.</p>
