<p>District N1 contains the northernmost reaches of Austin along I-35. It includes the Tech Ridge shopping center and the Walnut Creek Metropolitan Park.</p>

<p>The 2015 population estimate for District N1 is 31.489, according to the US Census Bureau's American Community Survey. About 56% of its 13,483 housing units are renter-occupied.</p>

<p>In District N1, property abatement cases dominate the neighborhood workload. Structure condition cases are the majority of cases for multifamily, and land-use violations lead commercial cases.</p>
