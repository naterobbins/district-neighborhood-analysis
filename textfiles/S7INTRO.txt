The S7 District is roughly bound by Mopac to the west and the Union Pacific Railroad to the east. S7 is home to the Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center and Bauerle Ranch Park. There are approximately 45,000 people that live in S7. The average household income in the district is about $95,000. There are almost 12,000 owner occupied housing units in S7. Nearly 80% of the district's residents are White, but there is also a large Hispanic population. Roughtly 60% of the S7's Code complaints are of the Property Abatement variety and roughly 20% are of the Land Use variety. Neighborhood cases make up over 90% of the district's caseload.
