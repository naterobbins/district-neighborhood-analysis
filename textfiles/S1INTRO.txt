The S1 District is basically the City of Austin land that is south of William Cannon Drive and east of IH 35. Onion Creek Country Club, Onion Creek Metropolitan Park, and Onion Creek Soccer Complex are all located within the distric boundaries. The population of S1 is roughly 30,000. Average household income in the district is around $63,000. There are about 6,000 owner occupied housing units in S1. Almost 2/3 of the district's residents are Hispanic and about 1/4 are White. Over 60% of the cases in S1 are Property Abatement cases. There tends to be an equal amount of Land Use cases and Structure Condition cases. Over 90% of the complaints in the district are Neighborhood complaints.
