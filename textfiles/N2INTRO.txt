<p>District N2 is in North Austin bounded by Lamar on the east, Metric on the west, Rutland on the south, and Kramer on the north.</p>

<p>Its estimated population is 12,248 based on the US Census Bureau's 2015 American Community Survey. About 65% of its 4049 housing units are renter-occupied.</p>

<p>In District N2, property abatement cases dominate the neighborhood workload. Structure condition cases are the majority of cases for multifamily, and commercial cases are mostly split between land-use and structure-condition cases.</p>
