The S6 District is primarily bound by Brodie Lane to the west, the Union Pacific Railroad to the west, William Cannon Drive to the north, and Davis Lane to the South. The Stephenson Nature Preserve And Outdoor Education Center takes up a significant portion of the district. There are approximately 13,000 people that live in S6. The average household income for the district is around $70,000. There are just over 3,000 owner occupied housing units in S6. Over 70% of the district's residents are White, and almost all of the remaining residents are Hispanic. Property Abatement violatons make up almost 60% of the caseload and over 25% of cases are of the Land Use variety. About 90% of complaints fall under Neighborhood.
