<p>District W5 is home to Camp Mabry, Mt. Bonnell, portions of Bull Creek District Park, and some smaller greenspaces. There are roughly 35,000 people that in W5. The majority of homes in the district are owned rather than rented. Residents in W5 tend to be in a higher income bracket than in other portions of the city. The majority of the district's residents are White, with a very small percentage of African American residents. Land-use violations are the most common and Work-without-permit are the least common violation types in W5. Neighborhood cases are far more prevalent than multi-family or commerical cases. On average, there tends to be a few more multi-family cases than there are commercial cases.</p>
