The majority of the W2 district is located between FM 620 and Capital of Texas Highway. This district is home to St. Edward's Park as well as Balcones Country Club. There are roughly 27,000 people that live in W2. The housing is split pretty evenly between owner-occupied and renter-occupied. The most common income bracket in this district in $50,000-$75,000. The district's residents are nearly 75% White. The Asian population in W2 is slightly larger than the Hispanic population. Land Use and Property Abatement cases are equally as common. Structure Conditon and Work Without Permit cases are less common in W2. Neighborhood cases makeup the vast majority of the district's case load, while Multi-family cases are very rare.
