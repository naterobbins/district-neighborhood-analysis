The majority of the W3 district is north of the intersection of Mopac and Research Blvd. The district features a sizeable retail between Mopac and Research Blvd. W3 is also home to the Riata residential community and corporate park. There are about 31,000 people that live in W3. Roughly 70% of the housing in this district is renter occupied. The most common income bracket in W3 is $50,000-$75,000. Around 75% of the residents are White, but there is a significant Hispanic populaton in this district. Property Abatement and Land Use are the most common violation types in W3. There tend to be a few more Property Abatement cases than Land Use cases, and Work Without Permit cases are scarce. The vast majority of complaints in W3 are of the Neighborhood variety, and the number of Commercial and Multi-family complaints are pretty equal, but far less common.
